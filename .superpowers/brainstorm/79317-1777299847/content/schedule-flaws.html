⚠ Known Flaws & Attack Vectors Not Covered

Flaw 1 — MQTT Broker Trust

Mosquitto has no auth by default. Anyone on same WiFi can publish to any topic. Fix: enable Mosquitto ACL + username/password. Out of scope for 3 days but real vulnerability.

Flaw 2 — car/command not authenticated

Hacker can also publish to car/command with malicious corrections. ESP32 has no way to verify message origin. Fix: HMAC signing (Day 3 bonus).

Flaw 3 — GPS is fully simulated

Hardcoded waypoints don't reflect real movement. Real GPS + real drift = harder to detect spoofing. Acceptable for demo scope but worth stating.

Flaw 4 — ML model not retrained

Isolation Forest trained once on 15 min data. Gradual drift attacks (slow speed increase over minutes) may not trigger it. Fix: sliding window retraining or LSTM.

Flaw 5 — Physical IR override

Hacker with a physical IR blaster can keep emitting GREEN signal. Hardware interrupt catches physical red but cannot distinguish legitimate vs spoofed IR sources.

Flaw 6 — Single point of failure

If laptop (broker + IDS + dashboard) crashes, car loses all cloud-side detection. No local fallback ML on ESP32. Fix: local Z-score threshold as fallback (Day 3 bonus).
